Our Story

Every Stitch Tells Your Pet's Story

WoolPet Portraits was born from a simple belief: the bond between a person and their pet deserves to be honoured in the most beautiful, tangible way possible. We blend centuries-old needle felting techniques with a keen eye for portraiture.

Our artisans spend 8–20 hours on every single portrait, hand-sculpting thousands of merino wool fibres into a lifelike, three-dimensional image that captures not just how your pet looks — but how they make you feel.

Every portrait arrives framed and ready to display, becoming a timeless heirloom that celebrates the joy and love only a pet can bring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order — answered honestly and in full.

Each portrait is made entirely by hand, and that care takes time. Our standard turnaround is 10–14 business days from the moment we receive your approved photo, plus shipping time to your location. For most destinations, tracked shipping adds 3–7 business days. If you need your portrait by a specific date — for a birthday, anniversary, or memorial — please mention it at checkout and we'll do everything we can to accommodate you. We also offer an express service (5–7 business days) for a small additional fee. You'll receive a preview image for your approval before we frame and dispatch, so nothing leaves our studio without your sign-off.

The ideal photo is clear, well-lit, and taken at your pet's eye level with their face fully visible and in focus. Natural daylight is best — flash tends to wash out coat colour and flatten texture, which are the very things we're working to recreate. That said, we know perfect pet photos are hard to come by. Don't let an imperfect photo put you off ordering. Our artists are experienced at working from a range of image qualities, and we'll always let you know if we need an additional angle or a supplementary shot before we start. We'd rather take a little extra time upfront than deliver a portrait that doesn't move you.

Your complete satisfaction is our absolute priority — and we mean that without caveat. Before your portrait is ever framed or shipped, we send you a high-resolution preview image for your approval. If anything isn't right — a colour, an expression, a background detail — we'll revise it. We offer unlimited complimentary revisions at the preview stage until you're genuinely thrilled. In the very rare event that an issue only becomes apparent on the physical piece, contact us within 14 days of delivery and we will remake or refund your order in full. No small print, no difficult conversations — just a commitment to getting it right.

Any clear photograph works, even older ones from a phone or printed photo that's been re-scanned. If you only have a single image, that's perfectly fine — we'll work with what you have and communicate openly if we need anything more. Many of our customers tell us that their memorial portrait has brought them real comfort in grief, and that knowledge is something we carry with us into every single piece we create. You're always welcome to include a note about your pet's personality — we love knowing who we're honouring.

Wool is a remarkably resilient and long-lasting natural fibre, and your portrait will remain beautiful for decades with minimal care. Keep it away from direct sunlight, which can gradually fade any artwork over time. Display it in a room with stable humidity — avoid bathrooms or areas prone to damp. Your portrait arrives behind UV-protective glass within its frame, which shields the fibres from light damage and dust. If the glass ever needs cleaning, use a dry or very lightly dampened soft cloth — never spray liquid directly onto the frame. Should any loose fibres appear over years of display, a very gentle pat with clean hands will settle them. With these simple precautions, your WoolPet Portrait is genuinely a lifelong heirloom.

Upon request, we can delicately incorporate your pet's real fur, hair, whiskers, or a small portion of ashes directly into the artwork. Typically, we blend their real fur with our premium merino wool to recreate the outer coat, or securely embed whiskers into the muzzle. This transforms your wool portrait into an incredibly intimate, tangible keepsake, just as discussed in the standards of our specialty manual "My Woolever Friend: Handmade Artistic Pet Replicas". Simply let us know your request when placing your order, and we will follow up with complete instructions.

Once your order is confirmed, we will provide you with a dedicated shipping address for our secure studio. To send fur or hair, place it inside a clean, dry, sealed Ziploc bag. Whiskers should be gently taped to a piece of rigid cardboard or stored inside a small, protective plastic sleeve to prevent bending during transit. For ashes, a tiny, securely sealed container (about half a teaspoon is plenty) inside a padded bubble mailer works best. We highly recommend using a tracked shipping service so you can monitor its journey. Rest assured, any unused portions or items will be returned to you along with your finished, framed portrait.
